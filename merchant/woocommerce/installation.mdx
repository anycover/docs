---
title: Plugin Installation
description: "Steps to install Anycover plugin on your Woocommerce store"
---


1.  Navigate to your WordPress admin dashboard
2.  Click **Plugins**
    ![](/images/woocommerce-install/1.png)
3.  Click **Add New**
    ![](/images/woocommerce-install/2.png)
4.  Download Anycover Plugin from [here](https://de03r9gaxziou.cloudfront.net/zip/Anycover-WC-Plugin-V1-0-3.zip)
    ![](/images/woocommerce-install/3.png)
5.  Click **Upload Plugin**
    ![](/images/woocommerce-install/4.png)
6.  Upload your plugin by drag/drop or choosing the file by clicking on choose file button
7.  Click the **Install Now**
    ![](/images/woocommerce-install/5.png)
8.  Click **Activate Plugin**
    ![](/images/woocommerce-install/6.png)
9.  Click **Approve**
    ![](/images/woocommerce-install/7.png)
10. Your Anycover Plugin should be properly installed
