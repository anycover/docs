---
title: App Installation
description: "Steps to install Anycover App on your Wix store"
---


1.  Navigate to your Wix site dashboard
2.  Click **Apps**
    ![](/images/wix-install/1.png)
3.  Search for "Anycover Extended Warranty" in the apps section
    ![](/images/wix-install/2.png)
4.  Click **Add to Site**
    ![](/images/wix-install/3.png)
5.  Check the permissions required and click **Agree & Add**
    ![](/images/wix-install/4.png)
6.  The above step will redirect you to Anycover onboarding pages wherein you can fill in all your contact details
7.  Read the Terms and Conditions and click **Next**
    ![](/images/wix-install/5.png)
8.  Fill in the information required for Primary, Technical, Billing, Claims Contacts
    ![](/images/wix-install/6.png)
9.  Choose the most appropriate statement
    ![](/images/wix-install/7.png)
10. Click "Next"
    ![](/images/wix-install/8.png)
11. Review the terms before clicking **Submit for Approval**
    ![](/images/wix-install/9.png)
